On the Subject of perspective eggs

(almost) clockwise eggs.

Write down the colors of the eggs in order starting with the egg that is 1 clockwise of the red egg, then continuing clockwise. After making this list, move the red color to the middle of the list.

Press the eggs in that color order. The eggs will move eratically around the module after the first press, hence why you wrote down the order first.
